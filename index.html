How To Play

Goal: Fill the entire board to win.

Move (desktop): Arrow keys.

Move (mobile): Swipe anywhere on the screen, or use the on-screen D-pad.

Pause (mobile): Tap anywhere on the screen, or use the Pause button / D-pad center.

Pause (desktop): Press Space.

Sideways mode (mobile): Turn your phone sideways. The board fills the screen; tap "Show Controls" in the top-right to bring up the D-pad when you need it, "Hide Controls" to give the board the space back.

Rules: Avoid walls and your snake body. Each food gives 1 point.

Continue: Resume from your last food after a short countdown.

Redo: Step back one food from Pause or Game Over.

Tip: on iOS/Android you can install Snake to your home screen for a fullscreen experience.
